Our Services

Governance that works under pressure. Ethics that hold when it matters most. Clarity. Confidence. A safer path to growth.

Overview

Specialist GRC Advisory

Airetris GRC provides specialist governance, risk, and compliance services for organizations operating under complexity, scrutiny, and consequence.

Our services are designed for moments when governance must work in practice, withstand regulatory and stakeholder scrutiny, and support strategic decision‑making — not just formal compliance.

We operate at board, executive, and enterprise level.

Solutions

What We Deliver

Board & Governance Services

We support boards and committees in exercising effective oversight in high‑risk and fast‑changing environments.

  • Board and committee governance frameworks
  • Terms of reference & governance design
  • Audit and risk committee support
  • Board effectiveness & training
  • Oversight of AI, cyber, and technology risks

Legal, Risk & Compliance

We help organizations simplify complex regulatory environments while strengthening control and accountability.

  • Corporate governance & legal operations
  • Regulatory compliance (EU, US, global)
  • Enterprise risk management & controls
  • ESG, sustainability & regulatory reporting
  • Policies, procedures & documentation

Investigations & Regulatory

Airetris supports organizations when trust, ethics, or controls are under pressure.

  • Internal and external investigations
  • Regulatory inquiries & enforcement response
  • Whistleblowing & misconduct matters
  • Crisis governance & remediation
  • Independent reviews & assurance

AI & Digital Ethics

Govern AI, data, and digital systems responsibly, defensibly, and in line with emerging regulatory expectations.

  • AI governance frameworks & oversight models
  • AI risk classification & model inventories
  • Data governance, privacy & cybersecurity
  • Human accountability & decision controls
  • Vendor & third‑party AI governance

IPO & M&A Readiness

Transactions expose governance weaknesses quickly. We support organizations before, during, and after major transactions.

  • Pre‑IPO governance & risk readiness
  • M&A due diligence (legal, ESG, IT, cyber)
  • Post‑transaction integration & remediation
  • Board and management readiness

Fractional Leadership

When permanent capability is not yet in place, we provide senior‑level expertise on a fractional or interim basis.

  • General Counsel / Chief Legal Officer
  • Chief Compliance Officer
  • Chief Risk Officer / ERM Lead
  • Chief Information Security Officer
  • Data Protection Officer / MLRO
  • Head of GRC
