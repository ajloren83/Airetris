Our Services

Governance that works under pressure. Ethics that hold when it matters most. Clarity. Confidence. A safer path to growth.

Overview

Specialist GRC Advisory

Airetris GRC provides specialist governance, risk, and compliance services for organizations operating under complexity, scrutiny, and consequence.

Our services are designed for moments when governance must work in practice, withstand regulatory and stakeholder scrutiny, and support strategic decision‑making — not just formal compliance.

We operate at board, executive, and enterprise level.

Solutions

What We Deliver

Board Governance

Elevating governance for strategic success.

We support boards and board committees across the full governance lifecycle, including:

  • Board ethics and culture
  • Governance structures and delegation of authority
  • Committee design, conflicts management, and succession planning
  • AI, cyber, and regulatory training for boards
  • Audit committee support and oversight

AI & Digital Ethics Governance

Navigating trust in a digital world.

We help boards and leadership teams govern AI and digital systems responsibly, with services including:

  • AI governance frameworks
  • Risk classification and control design
  • Data governance and privacy oversight
  • Human oversight and accountability models
  • Board-level AI literacy and training

CSDDD & Sustainability

Embedding responsibility into governance and value chains.

We support organisations in meeting CSDDD and sustainability due diligence obligations through:

  • CSDDD readiness assessments and gap analysis
  • Value chain and third-party due diligence
  • Sustainability governance and oversight design
  • Integration into ERM, GRC, and ESG frameworks
  • Monitoring, remediation, and board-level reporting

Pre- and Post-IPO Readiness

Strategic preparation and compliance for public listing.

We support organisations before and after listing through:

  • Disclosure governance frameworks
  • Insider and MNPI management
  • Board readiness for listed-company obligations
  • ESG and cyber readiness
  • Regulator and exchange liaison

Pre- and Post-M&A Readiness

Governing transactions with precision and alignment.

We support transactions and integrations through:

  • Legal, compliance, and ESG due diligence
  • Integration and migration planning
  • Post-deal governance redesign
  • Cultural and ethical alignment
  • Post-merger monitoring

Audit & Assurance

Independent insight. Board-ready assurance.

We provide independent assurance and monitoring services, including:

  • Risk-based audit planning
  • Program effectiveness reviews
  • CSDDD and ESG audits
  • AI and data governance audits
  • Audit committee reporting
  • Remediation tracking

Governance & ESG Reporting

From numbers to narrative.

We support high-quality reporting through:

  • Board and regulator reporting
  • ESG and sustainability disclosures
  • Compliance and investigations reporting
  • IPO and M&A status reporting

Fractional Leadership

Expert leadership, scalable and on-demand.

We provide senior, fractional leadership roles including:

  • Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)
  • Chief Risk Officer (CRO)
  • General Counsel (GC)
  • Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
  • Data Protection Officer (DPO)
  • Head of AI Ethics, ESG, and Investigations
  • Board Secretary and Ombudsman
